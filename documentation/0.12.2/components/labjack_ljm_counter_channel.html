LabJack LJM Counter Channel¶

Available on macOS

Description¶

Counter channel on a LabJack LJM Device.

Counts the rising edge of pulses on the selected digital line. Supports both high-speed and interrupt counters.

Signature¶

iochannel/labjack_ljm_counter

Required Parameters¶

line¶

Examples:
CIO1
fio6

Digital line name

value¶

Variable in which to store edge counts

Placement¶

Allowed at top level:

No

Allowed parent:

LabJack LJM Device
